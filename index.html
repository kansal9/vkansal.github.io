about me

I am a Researcher Engineer in the Astrophysics department. My field of research is Cosmology, in particular Weak Lensing. I work on observational data as well as N-Body simulations to constrain dark matter properties. My major focus is to evolve methods to measure the dark matter.

I have worked as a research engineer in the areas of space science, robotics, and management. I am self-motivated and passionate for the perfection and quality of the work. I have a ability to work independently and as a part of a team. I am always open for a new challenge and willing to update my knowledge and skills to enhance productivity. I have acquired extensive knowledge and experience in both scientific computing and engineering from my professional training.

Research Interests: Astrophysics, Cosmology, Dark Matter, Scientific Computing & Visualisation, Data Analysis.
